Schedule

Highly recommended that you follow this schedule on the trip

Mar 14

  1. Board the flight to Beijing $938 (Link), starting at 12:40 pm (travel time is roughly 11 hours 20 minutes from Vancouver)

Mar 15

  1. Arrive at Beijing Capital Airport, 4 PM local time.
  2. Take a taxi to Aman Summer Palace. Roughly 40 minutes travel time, estimated arrival time at 4:40 PM.
  3. Stay at the Summer Palace to rest and recuperate from jet lag.
  4. For dinner, eat takeout from nearby dumpling shop.

Mar 16

  1. Breakfast at hotel.
  2. Travel by limousine to the Great Wall of China for the day, $35.32
  3. Buy some street food for lunch.
  4. Come back in the afternoon/evening and shop around and buy some food.

Mar 17

  1. Breakfast at hotel.
  2. Go to Tiananmen Square by limo.
  3. Short break for shopping and eating.
  4. Go to Forbidden City, $18.
  5. For dinner, go to Quanjude Peking duck restaurant.

Mar 18

  1. Breakfast from hotel.
  2. Spend some time shopping for souvenirs and exploring the city.
  3. Lunch at Xian Lao Man.
  4. Go to Forbidden City again, $18
  5. Dinner at Lost Man Beijing.

Mar 19

  1. Breakfast from hotel.
  2. Travel by limo to the Temple of Heavens and surrounding attractions.
  3. Lunch at a small cafe of your choice.
  4. Go to Gubei Water Town until late night (to see the lights)
  5. Dinner at King's Joy.

Mar 20

  1. Breakfast from hotel.
  2. Go to Beihai Park.
  3. Walk to nearby Jingshan Park.
  4. Eat street food near the lake.
  5. Go to TRB Forbidden Palace for dinner.
  6. For the night, drive by limousine to Mandarin Oriental.

Mar 21

  1. Breakfast from hotel.
  2. Brunch/lunch at nearby restaurant or cafe.
  3. Beijing Zoo panda watching, $26.16.
  4. Bo Xing De Cafe for dinner.

Mar 22

  1. Breakfast from hotel.
  2. Eat at restaurants inside New Summer Palace.
  3. Go inside New Summer Palace ($7.85).
  4. Dinner at QuanDePing.

Mar 23

  1. Breakfast from hotel.
  2. Get on bus to Baipu Temple.
  3. Get takeout from nearby restaurant to eat at Baipu Temple.
  4. Dinner at Opera Bombana.

Mar 24

  1. Breakfast at hotel.
  2. Go to Old Summer Palace.
  3. Takeout from restaurant for lunch.
  4. Eat at Summer Palace for dinner.

Mar 25

  1. Breakfast from hotel.
  2. Check out from the hotel.
  3. Final drive around the entire city, lunch at The Georg.
  4. Go to the airport.
  5. Take flight back from Vancouver, departs at 6 pm. Arrive at Vancouver at 12pm Mar 25.
