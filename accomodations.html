Accomodations

Mandarin Oriental Hotel Wangfujing

A beautifully luxurious hotel filled with a mix of modern and traditional furniture, every room in Mandarin Oriental’s Wangfujing location is a work of art. Enjoy the carefully selected couple’s room, which includes a large king-sized bed laden with soft silk sheets, bright glass chandeliers, complimentary breakfast, and charming decor.

This hotel will be you and your spouse's home for five days, each with a delicious breakfast for absolutely free. The next five days will be at Aman Summer Palace’s extravagant guest room; see below for more info.

Aman Summer Palace Hotel

A grandeur hotel filled with traditional artwork and architecture; fresh flowers, gorgeous walls, and plush decor make this a very distinguished and luxurious tourist favourite. Comes with complimentary breakfast, small dining tables, and a perfectly relaxing atmosphere – good for a quiet but content stay.

Attached to a small river running through the compounds, you can view the dripping willows and cool moss from the comfort of your own room.

With one king-sized bed and a lavish marble bath opening onto a small courtyard, everything you want in a trip can be found here.
